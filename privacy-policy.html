Privacy Policy
  1. 1. Definitions

    1.1 Business User: A business entity or individual business owner that uses Internative platform to create and send content to their customers and contact base.

    1.2 End User: Any person or entity who receives communication from a Business User via the Internative platform.

    1.3 Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

    1.4 Cookies: Small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website, used for various purposes including tracking and analytics.

  2. 2. Collection of Personal Data

    2.1 Business Registration: When businesses sign up for Internative, they provide personal data such as their contact information. We collect this information to create and manage their accounts. Also, if they have given their consent to us, we send them promotional, marketing material and other notifications regarding their operations and usage on Internative Platform.

    2.2 Consent: By registering on Internative, businesses provide consent to our Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Policy. We store their contact data securely and may use it to send promotional messages and notifications related to their operations on the platform.

    2.3 End User Data: Internative enables businesses to send messages to their users and prospective customers using our email and WhatsApp capabilities. While we do not directly collect end user data, businesses are responsible for managing their databases, ensuring consent, and compliance with relevant laws.

    Information collected basis the consent given:-

    • Information You Provide Directly:
      • Business Users: When you sign up for the Platform, we collect contact information like name, email address, phone number, and company details.
      • All Users: We may collect information you provide through forms, surveys, feedback, or communication with us.
    • Information Collected Automatically:
      • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Platform, such as pages visited, time spent on the platform, and features used.
      • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
      • Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activity on the Platform.
    • Information from Third Parties:
      • We may receive information about Business Users from publicly available sources or third-party service providers we use.
      • We may receive information about users who receive communications from Business Users through the Platform, such as whether they opened an email or clicked on a link. However, we do not collect the content of those communications.
